The Aura Process

Every drop, intentional.

01

Source Rare Botanicals

We travel to 28 countries, selecting only the finest natural raw materials from ethical, sustainable farms and wild-harvest regions.

02

Compose by Master Nose

Our master perfumer, trained in Grasse, spends months composing each fragrance — balancing top, heart, and base notes into harmony.

03

Age & Macerate

Formulas are aged for a minimum of six weeks in oak barrels, allowing molecules to bind and the scent to reach full maturity.

04

Hand-fill & Seal

Each bottle is filled by hand in our atelier, sealed with 24-carat gold leaf, and presented in FSC-certified packaging.
